VaultX — about

A non-custodial marketplace for 1-of-1 collectibles: trading cards, digital art, fine wine, farm goods and antiques. Items are ERC-721 tokens; every trade settles on-chain with creator royalties baked in.

How it works

🔗

1 · Connect

Connect MetaMask on the VaultX Local network (chain 31337). Browsing needs no wallet — only buying, listing and withdrawing do.

🛒

2 · Trade

Buy at the listed price in one transaction. You always keep custody of your own items — the marketplace only moves an NFT at the moment of sale.

💸

3 · Earn & withdraw

Sale proceeds are held by the contract and pulled to your wallet when you click Withdraw — a safer pull-payment pattern.

Fees

Every sale splits three ways: a 2.5% platform fee, a 5% creator royalty (ERC-2981, paid to the original minter on every resale), and the remainder to the seller. The platform fee is capped on-chain at 10% and can never exceed it.
